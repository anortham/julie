### Verdict: concerns (3 findings)

**HIGH**: `cleanup.rs:204-242` prune_missing_workspaces uses a single-shot `Path::exists()` check at each 600s sweep tick. Any transient filesystem miss (NAS flake, unmounted drive, VPN drop) triggers `tokio::fs::remove_dir_all` on the entire Tantivy + SQLite + embeddings index. Idle workspaces have zero protection. No grace period, no error-kind discrimination.
