### A2.1 specifics worth remembering
- API: `WorkspaceConnectionPool::{new, with_limits, acquire,
  evict_idle, stats}` — public surface locked in advance, no scope
  creep.
- Mutex (std::sync, not parking_lot — no new dep) is **never** held
  across `.await` or file I/O. The acquire loop uses an
  `AcquireOutcome` enum to release the mutex before opening a new
  conn or awaiting Notify.
- `PooledConn: Send` is the load-bearing invariant for A2.2 — handlers
  will hold it across `.await` points.
- PRAGMA contract mirrors `SymbolDatabase::new` exactly (WAL + verify,
  busy_timeout=5000ms, synchronous=NORMAL, wal_autocheckpoint=2000).
  Factored into private `open_connection`; not extracted from
  database/mod.rs (out of scope).
- Idle eviction: stale-first, never below min. `now: Instant` is
  injectable so tests don't sleep.
