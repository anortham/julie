## Track 1 Task 3 restart handoff cleanup

- **WHAT:** Removed the adapter's fixed two-second sleeps and replaced them with explicit restart-handoff decisions plus a testable injected adapter loop.
- **WHY:** `run_adapter` still depended on timing folklore for restart handoff. That made stale-binary replacement and version-mismatch reconnects harder to reason about and left the retry policy untested.
- **HOW:** Added `RestartHandoffAction` and `restart_handoff_action()` in `src/daemon/lifecycle.rs`, extended `RestartReason` for adapter-observed handoff cases, and extracted `run_adapter_with()` in `src/adapter/mod.rs` so the retry loop can be exercised directly in tests. Added adapter retry tests that assert connect failure retry, immediate disconnect retry, and retry-budget exhaustion without any fixed delay.
- **IMPACT:** Adapter retry now follows named restart-handoff outcomes instead of sleeping and hoping. The daemon and adapter share one control-plane vocabulary for restart handoff, which sets up Task 4 dashboard/session surfacing cleanly.
