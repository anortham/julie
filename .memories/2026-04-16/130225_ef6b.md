## Track 1 Task 1 lifecycle extraction

- **WHAT:** Centralized daemon lifecycle state and restart decisions in `src/daemon/lifecycle.rs`, then routed `run_daemon` and `accept_loop` through those helpers instead of inline flag-toggling logic. Removed version-gate policy from `src/daemon/ipc_session.rs` so transport/session parsing no longer owns restart semantics.
- **WHY:** The daemon control plane had restart, drain, and state-file behavior scattered across `mod.rs` and `ipc_session.rs`, which made Windows stale-binary replacement and adapter handoff harder to reason about and harder to test.
- **HOW:** Added `LifecyclePhase`, `ShutdownCause`, `RestartReason`, transition helpers, and pure accept/disconnect decision helpers. `run_daemon` now writes lifecycle phases via `write_daemon_phase`, and `accept_loop` routes stale-binary and version-mismatch handling through lifecycle actions plus `flag_restart_pending_for_restart`. Updated daemon lifecycle/state/version-gate tests to assert lifecycle outcomes directly.
- **IMPACT:** Track 1 now has a single control-plane vocabulary for startup, ready, draining, restart-required, and stopping. This reduces duplicated restart logic and sets up Task 2 transport extraction without changing the adapter-visible `starting/ready/stopping` state-file contract.
