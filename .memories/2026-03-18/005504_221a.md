## Complete 15-Language Verification Sweep + Systemic type_usage Fix

### WHAT
Ran the full 8-check language verification checklist against all 15 Full Tier languages using parallel agents, identified systemic bugs, and fixed the highest-impact one (type_usage identifier gap) across 6 languages in parallel worktree agents.

### WHY
Julie supports 33 languages but had never been systematically verified beyond the first 4 (GDScript, Zig, TypeScript, QML/Razor). Needed comprehensive evidence of what works and what doesn't before claiming production readiness.

### HOW — The Parallel Approach
- **Phase 1:** Cloned 11 repos + reused 2 existing → 15 reference workspaces indexed
- **Phase 2:** Dispatched 13 verification agents in 3 batches (4+4+5), each running all 8 checks
- **Phase 3:** Compiled results, identified 3 systemic bug categories
- **Phase 4:** Dispatched 6 worktree agents to fix type_usage gap (Java, PHP, C, Kotlin, C++, Dart)
- **Phase 5:** Live-verified fixes (Gson: 0.00 → 1.00 centrality, Slim App: 0.00 → 0.24)
