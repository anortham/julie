## Candidate 8 line-enrichment observability

### WHAT
Added explicit line-enrichment trace metadata for fast_search content paths.

- Added `LineEnrichmentStatus` and companion fields on `SearchTrace`
- Recorded applied/no-match/failed enrichment outcomes for full-output snippets and locations-mode line hits
- Surfaced enrichment metadata through handler search telemetry
- Exposed line-mode zero-hit/file-pattern diagnostics on `LineModeSearchResult`
- Added regression coverage for success, no-match, locations trace status, telemetry serialization, enum serialization, and stale companion-field clearing
- Updated the cleanup plan and verification ledger

### WHY
Candidate 8 still had silent degradation: full-output line-mode enrichment swallowed errors, and locations fallback could silently return unified locations without saying line-mode failed or found no line matches.

### HOW
Kept ranking/execution behavior unchanged and added observability at the existing post-unified enrichment boundary. `SearchTrace` now owns small record helpers so call sites set status consistently. A fresh Claude review approved the slice and flagged low residuals; follow-up documented count semantics, clarified snippet scope-rescue behavior, and made failed enrichment clear stale companion fields.

### IMPACT
Search callers and telemetry can now distinguish successful line enrichment from no-match or failed line-mode fallback. Candidate 8's remaining work is the deeper structural integration of line matches into the unified execution plan, not the now-fixed observability gap.
