## Repaired Stage-Count Fixtures After Separator Matching

- **WHAT:** Updated two `line_mode_or_fallback_tests` fixtures so they keep testing file-pattern stage attribution after punctuation/separator matching changed.
- **WHY:** The old fixture lines used `marker abc` and `marker scope`, which now match `marker_abc` and `marker_scope` by design. That caused scope rescue to return hits and broke tests whose intent was to keep the fallback line matcher empty.
- **HOW:** Reversed the marker token order to `abc marker` and `scope marker`. Tantivy still sees both terms, but the line verifier does not accept the reversed sequence.
- **IMPACT:** Stage-count and no-in-scope diagnostics remain covered without fighting the new separator matching behavior.
