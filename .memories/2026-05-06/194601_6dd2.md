- Used Julie orientation and subagent exploration to identify symbol creation paths and AST pitfalls.

- Routed R special idioms through a small call/assignment dispatcher and text-argument helper module rather than expanding `r/mod.rs` further.
- Routed Elixir guard/delegate/exception/overridable forms through a dedicated `definition_forms` module and updated relationship filtering.
- Refreshed affected Dart and R golden fixtures after behavior changes.

### IMPACT
Task 8 is implemented and verified. Extractor output is more truthful for Dart metadata, much richer for R class systems and source imports, and no longer drops common Elixir definition forms.
