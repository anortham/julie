## Continued Phase 4 test modularization + Phase 3 stability checks

### WHAT
- Split oversized get_context test suite into focused modules under `src/tests/tools/`:
  - `get_context_graph_expansion_tests.rs`
  - `get_context_allocation_tests.rs`
  - `get_context_token_budget_tests.rs`
- Removed duplicated/moved modules from `src/tests/tools/get_context_tests.rs`
- Updated module wiring in `src/tests/mod.rs`
- Confirmed `src/tests/tools/get_context_tests.rs` now reduced to **863 lines** (previously 1336), back under the <=1000 test guideline.

### WHY
- Project standards require test files to stay <=1000 lines.
- The original get_context test file had become a large mixed-suite file, making maintenance and navigation painful.

### HOW
- Extracted graph expansion, allocation, and token-budget tests into dedicated files with isolated helper scaffolding.
- Kept behavior/test names intact where possible to preserve intent and historical signal.
- Ran targeted suites for each extracted module plus aggregate get_context and fast-tier validation.

### IMPACT
- Test organization now aligns with project policy and is easier to evolve.
- Reduced risk of merge conflicts and cognitive load when changing specific get_context subareas.
- Preserved full green state after refactor.
