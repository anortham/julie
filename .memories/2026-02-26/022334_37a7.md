## Approved design for search-layer NL relevance improvements

Captured and documented an approved design to fix natural-language relevance gaps at the **search layer** rather than only in `get_context`.

- **What:** Added a design doc at `docs/plans/2026-02-25-search-layer-nl-relevance-design.md`
- **Why:** `get_context` fallback cannot recover when retrieval returns docs/tests-only candidates
- **How:** Chosen deterministic query expansion + mild NL-only path priors, preserving existing AND->OR and field boosts
- **Impact:** Establishes implementation direction with bounded risk and clear TDD regression guards

Includes architecture, alternatives, guardrails, TDD plan, verification commands, and success criteria.
