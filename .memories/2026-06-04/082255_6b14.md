## U3 Complete: julie-pipeline crate created, both gates green

### WHAT
Created `crates/julie-pipeline` crate and relocated `indexing_core/**`, `embeddings/**`, `resolver` modules, `resolve_pending_relationships`, and all associated tests from the top-level `julie` crate.

### WHY
Phase 2a of the Julie Rescue plan — extract the pipeline layer into a leaf crate to reduce relink tax. Crate DAG: `julie-core → julie-index → julie-pipeline → julie`.

### HOW
Pure relocation with import-path fixups:
- `crate::*` → `julie_core::*`, `julie_index::*`, `julie_extractors::*`
- `include_dir!` path adjusted: `$CARGO_MANIFEST_DIR/../../python/...`
- `include_str!` path adjusted: `../../../../python/...`
- Top-level shims added: `src/indexing_core/mod.rs`, `src/embeddings/mod.rs`, `src/utils/walk.rs`, `src/tools/workspace/indexing/resolver.rs` all re-export from `julie_pipeline::*`
