## Recalibrated the get-symbols bucket safety timeout

`tools-get-symbols` exhausted its 120-second cumulative ceiling after three valid commands consumed about 109 seconds, leaving too little time for the fourth command.

- **WHAT:** Raised only the bucket timeout from 120 to 300 seconds while preserving its 40-second declared planning estimate and the dev tier's 568-second declared total.
- **WHY:** Under the current environment the five commands complete successfully in 140 seconds; repeated timeout kills orphaned two test binaries and their test-owned sidecars until they were explicitly terminated.
- **HOW:** TDD proved the manifest contract rejected the old timeout, then accepted 300 seconds. The exact bucket passed all five commands and all 179 xtask tests stayed green.
- **IMPACT:** The dev gate can complete valid get-symbols coverage without exceeding the tier's under-ten-minute declared budget or leaving timed-out child processes behind.
