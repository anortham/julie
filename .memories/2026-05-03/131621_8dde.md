## Implemented Changed-Selection Rationale

### WHAT

Added explicit rationale output for `cargo xtask test changed` bucket selection and dev fallback decisions.

### WHY

Agents need to know why `changed` selected a bucket or fell back to `dev`, otherwise the runner still behaves like a black box and verification ledger entries are hard to trust.

### HOW

- Added `rationale` lines to `ChangedSelection`.
- Recorded path-to-bucket rationale for normal bucket mode.
- Recorded exact-file, prefix, manifest-level, and unknown fallback reasons for fallback-to-dev mode.
- Kept docs/memory ignored-path output concise for no-run diffs.
- Added changed-selection contract tests and trimmed an unnecessary `main.rs` no-op edit during lead review.

### IMPACT

Task 2 of the testing-contract plan is complete. `changed` output can now be pasted into ledger evidence with enough detail to explain fallback and bucket routing decisions.
