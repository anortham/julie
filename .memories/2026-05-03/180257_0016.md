## Added Workspace Session Attachment Service

- **WHAT:** Added `WorkspaceSessionAttachment` as a thin service for session workspace attachment and routed handler attach paths through it.
- **WHY:** The workspace service split needs a single place for session/workspace attachment before watcher refs and session-count side effects are moved out of `WorkspacePool`.
- **HOW:** Added an idempotent `attach_workspace_once` API, a sync-indexed variant for activation, and a direct attached-ID marker for loaded-workspace swaps. Updated handler paths for primary attach, loaded workspace swap marking, and explicit activation.
- **IMPACT:** The service now owns attach idempotence at handler boundaries while preserving existing pool side effects for the next migration slice.
