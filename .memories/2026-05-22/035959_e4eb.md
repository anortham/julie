## T8: Atomic Cutover â€” `search_target` Removed from Public Surface

### WHAT

### WHY
Phase 2 goal: unify the FTS index and remove the `search_target` routing discriminant from the public API. All queries (symbol lookup, content search, file-name search) now go through one path.
