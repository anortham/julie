### Key Design Decisions
- **Pre-computed at index time** (not on-demand) — single UPDATE with correlated SUM(CASE...)
- **Weighted by kind:** Calls=3, Implements/Imports/Extends/Instantiates=2, Uses/References=1, Contains=0
- **Logarithmic scaling:** `score *= 1.0 + ln(1 + ref_score) * 0.3` — compresses 3597:1 raw range to 3.46:1 boost range
- **Post-search boost** in tool layer (not Tantivy custom scorer), matching `apply_important_patterns_boost` pattern
- **Self-references excluded** from scoring
