## Task 6: julie-test-support crate extraction — complete

### WHAT
Extracted handler-free test helpers into a new `crates/julie-test-support` crate. Moved DB row builders, `open_test_connection`, `unique_temp_dir`, and `atomic_cleanup_julie_dir` out of the top `julie` crate. Original top-crate files became thin re-export shims.

### WHY
Phase 0 of Julie Rescue: isolating test helpers into a leaf crate so editing them doesn't trigger a full relink of the `julie` binary.

- Created `crates/julie-test-support/src/tempdir.rs` (verbatim copy)
- Created `crates/julie-test-support/src/cleanup.rs` (adapted; safety check 1 guards test-support manifest dir)
