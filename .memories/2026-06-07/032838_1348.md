## Fixed Julie 7.13.3 OpenClaw indexing regression

- **WHAT:** Reduced Julie's OpenClaw forced standalone index time from hanging past the 3-minute budget to `164.4s` with embeddings disabled, while producing `13,494` files, `565,029` symbols, and `185,010` relationships.
- **WHY:** The Miller/Julie/Eros search-quality bake-off could not produce valid OpenClaw Julie numbers because Julie 7.13.3 was CPU-bound after the julie-extractors/schema bump.
- **HOW:** Fixed three scaling issues found during diagnosis: moved `ExtractedBatch` into projection to avoid cloning huge vectors, deferred identifier secondary indexes during fresh full bulk insert, and replaced resolver identifier-presence cross-product/prefix SQL with temp-table exact joins plus exact scoped identifier presence.
- **IMPACT:** Julie now completes OpenClaw indexing within the expected budget and the search-quality runner produced valid Miller-vs-Julie OpenClaw scores. Eros OpenClaw remains blocked separately: its workspace canonical DB is empty and hub search fails with `Table 'search_docs' was not found`.
