## Unified extractor enrichment normalization and persistence

Julie now consumes the v2.16 extraction domains through one normalized data contract shared by full indexing, external extraction, and watcher updates.

- **Why:** Task 2 exposed typed SQLite write slices, but the batch and watcher still passed empty source-region, structural-fact, and complexity-metric slices.
- **How:** Added `NormalizedExtractionData` and `normalize_extraction_results`, moved literal-carrier classification, test-role classification, type conversion, and type-argument flattening into that seam, and carried all three new domains through `ExtractedBatch` and `WatcherExtractionWrite`.
- **Behavior preserved:** Pending and structured pending relationships, parse diagnostics, literals, test roles, types, and type arguments remain available to their existing consumers.
- **Impact:** Real v2.16 Rust extraction now persists documentation regions, `http.client_request.v1` facts, and per-symbol complexity; watcher replacement atomically removes stale enrichment rows.
